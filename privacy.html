Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

At Aditya Medical & General Store, we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and utilize your data when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect details necessary to process your health and product orders, including your name, contact phone number, delivery address, email, and any medical prescriptions you voluntarily upload for verification or AI analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your uploaded prescriptions are processed locally and securely stored in our databases solely for pharmacist verification and fulfillment of your requested medical orders. We do not sell, rent, or share your medical or contact details with third-party advertising companies.

3. Data Security & Storage

We utilize secure Supabase databases and encrypted transmission protocols to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your healthcare data. You can request the deletion of your account and personal history at any time by contacting us.
