Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Contact us if you need further help.

❓ Are the medicines sold at Aditya Medical genuine?

Yes, absolutely. We source all medicines and medical supplies directly from certified pharmaceutical distributors and reputable manufacturing labs. 100% authenticity is guaranteed.

❓ Do you deliver everywhere?

Yes, we offer fast same-day home delivery across all locations . For orders placed before 6:00 PM, delivery is completed within 2-4 hours.

❓ Is a prescription required to order medicines?

A valid medical prescription is required for Schedule H and prescription-only medicines. Over-the-counter (OTC) drugs, supplements, and device items do not require a prescription.

❓ How does the prescription upload feature work?

You can upload a photo or PDF of your prescription on our "Upload Prescription" page. Our local AI parses the text, and our certified pharmacist reviews it, loads the medicines into your order queue, and communicates price estimates directly.

❓ What are your store hours?

Aditya Medical & General Store is open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Online orders and prescription uploads can be placed 24/7, and they will be verified during store hours.
